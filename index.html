Code of Conduct

Server Rules

01

🤝 Respect All Players

Harassment, bullying, toxic behavior, or disrespect toward others is not permitted — even if they play on Bedrock.

02

🚫 No Hate Speech or Discrimination

Racism, sexism, or discriminatory language toward any individual or group is strictly prohibited.

03

⚖️ No Drama or Disruptive Behavior

Arguments, instigation, or unnecessary conflict may result in moderation action.

04

🔞 No NSFW or Spam

Posting NSFW content or excessive spam is not allowed in any channel or in-game.

05

🛑 No Cheats, Exploits, or Hacks

Cheat clients, advantage-giving mods, and item/entry duplication exploits are banned. Violations result in an immediate ban. Exceptions may apply to devs or owners fixing bugs.

06

🎭 No Impersonation

Impersonating staff, developers, or other members is prohibited.

07

🤖 Do Not Abuse Server Systems

Attempting to exploit server features or mechanics will result in a ban.

⚠️

Enforcement Policy

6–7 warnings may result in a temporary suspension (6–7 days) or permanent removal. Severe violations may result in immediate action without prior warnings. Staff decisions are final.
